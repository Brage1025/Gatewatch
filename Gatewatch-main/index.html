The Gatewatch is a group of planeswalkers initially allied together to fight the Eldrazi on Zendikar. Named for their defense of the Sea Gate. Their commitment is to protect the people of the Multiverse from threats that no one else can handle.

Jace Beleren

Mind-mage

Jace Beleren is a human planeswalker and mind-mage from Vryn. While working with the Infinite Consortium on Ravnica, he went by the name Berrim. He is the former Living Guildpact of Ravnica and a founding member of the Gatewatch. He was compleated during the events of Phyrexia: All Will Be One and remained a Phyrexian for some time until Elesh Norn's defeat.

Chandra Nalaar

Pyromancer

Chandra Nalaar, is a passionate human pyromancer from Kaladesh, she discovered her fiery magic while defying her world’s oppressive rules. After accidentally killing guards and losing her family, her planeswalker spark ignited, sending her across planes. Guided by emotion and freedom, she joined the Gatewatch to fight tyranny, learning to balance her impulsive fire with compassion and responsibility across the Multiverse.

Nissa Revane

Animist

Nissa Revane, is a Joraga Zendikari elf and former planeswalker who wields magic to muster elves and elementals and amplify their might. She was a member of the Gatewatch, before she was compleated during the events of Phyrexia: All Will Be One. At the end of New Phyrexia's Invasion of the Multiverse, she was un-compleated, and subsequently lost her spark as part of the consequences of the invasion.

Gideon Jura

Hieromancer

Gideon Jura, born Kytheon Iora, was a human hieromancer and planeswalker from Theros. After his reckless defiance led to his friends’ deaths, his spark ignited, sending him across the Multiverse. Guided by justice, he became a steadfast planeswalker and member of the Gatewatch, ultimately sacrificing himself on Ravnica to protect his allies and uphold his ideals.

Liliana Vess

Necromancer

Countess Liliana Vess is a human planeswalker from Dominaria and is both a master necromancer and a healer. For a while, she was dead to the Multiverse, her survival known only to select members of the Gatewatch. During the Phyrexian invasion, Liliana stood with the coalition, using her necromancy to turn Phyrexia’s dead against them. After the war, she retreated from the spotlight, continuing her pursuit of redemption as Professor Serafina Onyx at Strixhaven, before reclaiming her original name, and began taking steps to reclaim her ancestral Dominarian home.

Ajani Goldmane

Warrior

Ajani Goldmane, a leonin warrior from Naya, began his journey seeking justice for his brother’s murder, his spark igniting through grief and rage. Guided by compassion and wisdom, he became a healer, mentor, and steadfast protector across the planes, standing as a moral anchor within the Gatewatch. During the Phyrexian invasion, Ajani was tragically compleated by Elesh Norn, becoming one of her most devastating weapons. Freed only after her downfall, he was left broken and stripped of his spark, burdened by guilt yet determined to atone.

Teferi Akosa

Chronomancer

Teferi Akosa, a brilliant human chronomancer from Zhalfir, mastered time magic as a prodigy of the Tolarian Academy. His curiosity and confidence once led to disaster when his experiments phased his homeland out of time, burdening him with guilt for centuries. Seeking redemption, he joined the Gatewatch and became one of the Multiverse’s greatest strategists and leaders. During the Phyrexian invasion, Teferi returned to the present, bringing Zhalfir back from exile and leading its people against New Phyrexia. His mastery of time turned the tide of battle, though the effort cost him dearly—his Planeswalker spark fading after the war.

Kaya Cassir

Ghost-Assassin

Kaya Cassir, a skilled ghost assassin from an unknown plane, discovered her spark after a traumatic encounter with spirits and death magic. Trained in stealth and spectral manipulation, she became a relentless hunter of ghosts and undead, crossing planes in pursuit of justice and vengeance. During the Phyrexian invasion of the Multiverse, Kaya fought alongside the Gatewatch, using her unique ethereal powers to disrupt Phyrexian forces. Though she survived the war, her battles left her scarred and cautious, forever marked by the lives she saved and the deaths she could not prevent.

The New Phyrexia Strike Force

A ten-planeswalker* strike force team that infiltrated New Phyrexia, lead by Jace Beleren.

The strike force consisted of: Jace Beleren, Vraska, Kaya, The Wanderer, Kaito Shizuki, Nissa Revane, Lukka, Nahiri, Koth of the Hammer, Tyvar Kell.

Jace, Lukka, Nahiri, Nissa, and Vraska were compleated in the assault.

*Already mentioned planeswalkers are not listed below:

Elspeth Tirel

Archangel

Elspeth Tirel, a human born on Serra’s realm, endured a tragic childhood before her planeswalker spark ignited. She became a skilled warrior and champion of the innocent, traveling across planes to fight injustice. Branded a tool by the god Heliod after being imprisoned in the Helvault, she eventually escaped. During New Phyrexia’s invasion of the Multiverse, she confronted the Phyrexian forces and sacrificed herself. Through Serra’s grace, she was reborn as an archangel, continuing to battle Phyrexian corruption across the Multiverse until the threat was finally ended.

Kaito Shizuki

Cyber Ninja

Kaito Shizuki is a human ninja, born on Kamigawa and orphaned after a lab accident. While training in the Imperial Palace he befriended a young girl known as The Wanderer. When The Wanderer’s spark ignited after a devastating attack, Kaito left the palace to track the mysterious planeswalker Tezzeret and uncover the truth. His spark awakened alongside the kami Himoto, launching him into a path of ninja-infiltration and futurist ambition. He later confronted Jin-Gitaxias’ betrayal, joined efforts against the Phyrexian invasion, and helped defend Kamigawa.

Lukka

Solider

Lukka, a soldier from Ikoria, discovered his rare bond with monsters through the magical ability of bondmanship. Branded a traitor by his own military for this power, he turned against his homeland and embraced survival through strength. His planeswalker spark ignited amid betrayal, leading him across planes. During the infiltration he made the mistake to bond with a Phyrexian beast. This lead him to eventually become compleated and later killed by Vadrok and Vivien Reid during the siege of Ikoria as part of New Phyrexia's Invasion of the Multiverse.

Nahiri

Lithomancer

Nahiri is a kor lithomancer and previous planeswalker. She masters powerful stone magic and traveled the Multiverse following ancient grievances and betrayals, often clashing with other planeswalkers. She encountered the Gatewatch multiple times, alternately aiding and opposing them as her goals shifted. During the Phyrexian invasion of the Multiverse, she confronted their corruption, but was eventually compleated and lost her planeswalker spark, her powers twisted to serve New Phyrexia. After the Phyrexians were defeated, Nahiri was freed from their control, regaining her autonomy, though forever changed by the ordeal.

Tyvar Kell

Transmuter

Tyvar Kell, the elven Prince of Kaldheim and brother to King Harald, sought strength through unity and honor. Gifted with the ability to turn his skin to metal and adapt to any environment, his spark ignited through his drive to protect his people. Joining the Gatewatch, he fought bravely during the Phyrexian invasion of the Multiverse, standing beside heroes like Kaya and Kaito. Though he narrowly resisted compleation, Tyvar emerged changed by the war — his idealism tempered by loss alongside the loss of his planeswalker spark.

Vraska

Assassin

Vraska is a gorgon from Ravnica, rose from an assassin in the Golgari Swarm to its rightful queen. Betrayed and imprisoned by the Azorius, her planeswalker spark ignited, fueling her pursuit of freedom and power. She later allied with Jace Beleren, forming a complex bond that balanced ambition and empathy. During the Phyrexian invasion, Vraska was compleated and lost herself to the corruption. Though freed after New Phyrexia’s defeat, she was left powerless and broken, her memories and planeswalker spark gone, struggling to rebuild her identity in a world forever changed.

The Wanderer

Warrior

The Wanderer, once the young emperor of Kamigawa, lost control of her unstable spark after a catastrophic attack on her palace, vanishing between planes. Guided by duty and grace, she became a drifting protector, appearing across worlds to combat rising threats. Reuniting with her loyal friend Kaito Shizuki, she helped defend Kamigawa and later joined the fight against New Phyrexia. During the invasion, she confronted Elesh Norn directly, leading the charge that ended the Phyrexian tyranny, though lost her planeswalker spark after the war.

Koth of the Hammer

Geomancer

Koth of the Hammer, a Vulshok geomancer from Mirrodin, became a symbol of resistance after witnessing his home’s fall to Phyrexia. Wielding power drawn from the plane’s metallic core, he fought tirelessly to defend his people, leading the last Mirran survivors in a desperate war for freedom. His spark ignited through defiance, carrying him across planes in search of allies. During the Phyrexian invasion of the Multiverse, Koth returned to New Phyrexia to aid the coalition forces, standing alongside Elspeth and the Gatewatch. Though scarred by endless loss, he survived the conflict and remained on Mirrodin—now cleansed of Phyrexian rule he vows to rebuild his shattered world.
